Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1lbs butter
  • 2lbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs an cream in abowl
  3. once butter has melted add cream and eggs.
  4. Using a spatula fold teh eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat
